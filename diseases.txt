ADHD
    symptoms:
        daydream a lot
        forget or lose things a lot
        squirm or fidget
        talk too much
        make careless mistakes or take unnecessary risks
        have a hard time resisting temptation
        have trouble taking turns
        have difficulty getting along with others
        
        limited attention
        hyperactivity
    treatment options:
        (behavior therapy)
        (medication)
        combination of behavior therapy and medication

    symptoms:

        pain
        swelling
        reduced range of motion
        stiffness
    treatments:
        self-care
            heating pad

            weight loss
            tai chi
            yoga
            cold compress
            ice pack
        therapies
            hydrotherapy
            stretching
            massage
            acupuncture
        medications
            nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug
            steroid
            analgesic
            narcotic
            immunosuppressive drug
        surgery
            hip replacement

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)
    symptoms
        difficulty communicating and interacting with others
        repetitive behaviors as well as limited interests or activities
        symptoms that typically are recognized in the first two years of life
            high risk factors
                sister, brother, or other family member with ASD
                have some ASD behaviors
                born premature, early, and at a low birth weight
            evaluation by team of doctors and other health professionals
        symptoms that hurt the individual's ability to function socially, at school or wat work, or other areas of life
            getting upset by a slight change in a routine or being placed in a new or overly stimulating setting
            making little or inconsistent eye contact
            having a tendency to look at and listen to other people less often
    treatment options
Avian Influenza
    symptoms
        pain areas: in the muscles
        whole body: fever or malaise
        nasal: runny nose or sneezing
        coughing
        headache
        shortness of breath
        sore throat
        fever
    treatment options
        antiviral drug (within two days of symptoms)
